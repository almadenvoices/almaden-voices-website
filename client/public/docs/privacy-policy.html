Privacy Policy

Almaden Voices

Last Updated: January 2026

Almaden Voices ("we," "our," or "us") values the privacy of our students, families, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

A. Information Provided by Parents/Guardians

When registering a student or contacting us, parents/guardians may provide:

  • Parent/guardian name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Student name and grade
  • Emergency contact information
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment platforms)

B. Student Information

We may collect limited student information necessary for participation:

  • First name
  • Grade level
  • Participation in programs or events

C. Media (Photos & Videos)

With parent/guardian permission, we may collect:

  • Student video testimonials
  • Photos or recordings from classes, showcases, or events

These may be used on:

  • The Almaden Voices website
  • Program materials
  • Social media pages

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Register students for programs
  • Communicate with families about sessions, schedules, and events
  • Provide learning experiences
  • Share student achievements (with permission)
  • Improve our programs and website

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

3. Media Consent

Student photos or videos will only be shared publicly with parent or guardian consent. Parents may request removal of media at any time by contacting us.

4. Payments

Payments are processed through secure third-party providers (such as PayPal, Stripe, or similar services). Almaden Voices does not store full credit card information.

5. How We Protect Information

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information by:

  • Limiting access to authorized individuals
  • Using secure platforms for communication and registration
  • Avoiding collection of unnecessary data

However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Almaden Voices works with minors in educational settings. We only collect student information with parent/guardian involvement and consent.

7. Your Choices

Parents/guardians may:

  • Request to review or update their information
  • Ask for their child's media to be removed
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Almaden Voices grows. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
